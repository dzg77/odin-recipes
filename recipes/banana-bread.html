Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This moist and flavorful banana bread is the perfect way to use up overripe bananas, turning them into a sweet, comforting treat that is perfect for breakfast, a snack, or even dessert. The bananas add natural sweetness and moisture, creating a dense yet soft loaf that is full of rich flavor. Combined with a hint of vanilla and a sprinkle of cinnamon, each bite is a nostalgic, melt-in-your-mouth experience. Whether you enjoy it fresh out of the oven, toasted with a pat of butter, or with a cup of coffee, this banana bread is a timeless favorite.

What makes this banana bread even better is its versatility. You can easily customize it by adding chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits for extra texture and flavor. It is an easy-to-make recipe that does not require any fancy ingredients — just a few pantry staples and ripe bananas. Plus, the aroma that fills the kitchen as it bakes is irresistible. It is a great recipe for beginner bakers, as the steps are simple, and the result is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar (you can reduce this for less sweetness)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. Mash the bananas in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in the melted butter.
  3. Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add the baking soda, salt, and cinnamon (if using) to the mixture. Stir until incorporated.
  5. Add the flour and mix until just combined — be careful not to overmix.
  6. Fold in any optional add-ins like walnuts or chocolate chips, if desired.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and enjoy!
